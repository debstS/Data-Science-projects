Hello Visitor!

Debashree Tripathy is a hyper-curious Customer Data Scientist with more than 3 years of experience in Data analytics, business intelligence, and now Data Science.

Along with the professional expertise involving Data science, data analysis and engineering, she brings with herself an infectious energy to tell a story though the data, for unravelling the most promising solution beneath the shiny labels of 'machine learning' and 'artificial intelligence'.

She believes in finding the 'Ikigai' in every act - the sense of a bigger purpose behind the work we do. This has taken her quest to numerous geographies globally, across multiple industry domains, starting as a Database Developer with a global automobile giant, to being a Data Analyst on a pharmaceutical project with a Big 4 Consulting firm, and now as a Customer Data Scientist with an award-winning Property Technology organization in the UK.

Her journey gets more interesting with the fact that this time, on a quest to discover a life and a career which serves a greater purpose, she has now moved continents, did a Master's to upskill, and has now found her newfound love for Geospatial data science (and NLP at times!)

Academically, she holds a Bachelor’s degree from one of the top-ranked Deemed Universities in India, and a recent Master’s degree in Data Science with Distinction from the esteemed University of Manchester, which is ranked 27th globally. Motivation wise, she hopes to be a part of organizations such as National Geographic and United Nations someday, using Data Science for the good of the world, solving one critical problem at a time.

When not bookmarking stuff and forgetting to ever visit them, she will be found out and about on a foot trail, or binging on a carrot & walnut cake at the local Costa!
